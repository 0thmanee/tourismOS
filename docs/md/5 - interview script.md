# 🎯 Interview Script (Tourism Operators)

## 🟢 1. Introduction (Very Important)

Hi, I’m working on a project to better understand how tourism businesses operate in Morocco.
I’m not selling anything — I just want to learn from your experience.

---

## 🟢 2. Context (Understand their business)

* What kind of activities do you offer?
* How long have you been doing this?
* How many clients do you usually get per week?

---

## 🟢 3. Booking Workflow (CRITICAL SECTION)

* How do customers usually contact you?
* What happens from the first message until the booking is confirmed?
* How do you manage your schedule and availability?

---

## 🟢 4. Pain Points (MOST IMPORTANT)

* What is the most stressful part of your work?
* When do you lose clients?
* Do you ever have double bookings or confusion?
* What wastes your time the most every day?

---

## 🟢 5. Tools & Current Solutions

* What tools do you use today? (WhatsApp, Excel, etc.)
* Have you ever tried a booking system?
* If yes, why did you stop using it?

---

## 🟢 6. Money & Value

* If there was a tool that solves these problems, would you pay for it?
* How much would that be worth per month for you?

---

## 🟢 7. Trust & Customers

* Do tourists trust you easily?
* Do they hesitate before booking?
* Do scams or fake competitors affect your business?

---

## 🟢 8. Closing

Thank you, this really helps me understand the reality of the business.

---

# ⚠️ Important Behavioral Rules (Read this carefully)

### ❌ NEVER DO

* Don’t pitch your idea
* Don’t say “I’m building an app”
* Don’t lead them to answers

### ✅ ALWAYS DO

* Let them talk 70% of the time
* Ask follow-ups like:

  * “why?”
  * “how?”
  * “give me an example”

---

# 🧠 Pro Tips (Very important for Morocco)

### 1. Speak simple Darija

No formal Arabic. No French unless they start it.

---

### 2. Go physically if possible

Best locations:

* Marrakech (top priority)
* Taghazout (surf operators)
* Fes (guides)
* Agafay / desert tours

---

### 3. Watch behavior, not just answers

If they say:

> “everything is fine”

But you see:

* 3 phones
* notebook
* chaos

→ that’s the real truth.

---

### 4. Look for THESE signals

You found gold if you hear:

* I lose clients
* I mess up bookings
* everything on WhatsApp
* no visibility
