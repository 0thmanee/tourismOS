# 🎭 1. Simulated Real Interviews (Based on Market Reality)

These are **very close to what you’ll actually hear in Morocco**.

---

## 🧑‍🌾 Profile 1 — Desert Tour Operator (Merzouga)

Customers come from Instagram or Booking or referrals.
They message me on WhatsApp and I reply.
Sometimes I’m busy and I reply late → I lose them.
Sometimes I mess up bookings because I don’t track them properly.
Everything is in my head or WhatsApp.

---

### 🧠 Insight

* ❗ Lost revenue due to slow replies
* ❗ No system of record
* ❗ Memory-based operations

👉 This is a **HIGH VALUE PAIN**

---

## 🏄 Profile 2 — Surf School (Taghazout)

We get many messages, sometimes I lose track of who booked.
I only use WhatsApp and a notebook.
There’s a problem with organization, especially on weekends.
I tried a system but it was too complicated.

---

### 🧠 Insight

* ❗ Volume overload problem
* ❗ Tool adoption failure (too complex)
* ❗ Peak-time chaos

👉 Opportunity: **simple UX wins**

---

## 🏍️ Profile 3 — Quad Operator (Marrakech / Agafay)

The biggest problem is coordination.
I need to know who is coming, who goes with who, who will drive.
Sometimes we have team confusion.
I don’t have any dashboard or clear system.

---

### 🧠 Insight

* ❗ Staff coordination problem
* ❗ Multi-resource scheduling
* ❗ No visibility

👉 This is **operations software territory (strong SaaS)**

---

## 🧑‍🍳 Profile 4 — Cooking Workshop (Marrakech)

Customers ask a lot before booking.
I spend too much time replying.
Sometimes they don’t complete the booking.
I don’t have a proper payment system.

---

### 🧠 Insight

* ❗ Time wasted in repetitive conversations
* ❗ Low conversion rate
* ❗ Payment friction

👉 Opportunity: **automation + deposits**

---

## 🏕️ Profile 5 — Small Tour Agency

I work with many people: guides, drivers, hotels.
Coordination is hard.
Everything is on WhatsApp.
Sometimes there’s confusion in timing or planning.

---

### 🧠 Insight

* ❗ Multi-party coordination problem
* ❗ No centralized system
* ❗ High error risk

👉 This is **B2B infrastructure opportunity**
